About Dev's Judicial Academy

Welcome to Dev's Judicial Academy

The vision behind the endeavor was to provide a guiding light to the students preparing for Judicial Competitive Exams and Law Entrance/CLAT for various universities. The institute offers Classroom classes as well as Online classes and correspondence material for law and judicial exams. The main motive is not just to teach and complete the syllabus; rather to give students a bigger picture of life so that they are prepared for anything that comes across them. As a coaching institute we have the responsibility not only for teaching the subjects; rather producing the best officers, not only the best in their official duties but in their societal and National duties.

In our Academy, we are teaching all the aspirants who want to appear in the above examinations. He makes the concepts of law to each and every student crystal clear by explaining in detail each and every provision of the following topics of the said examinations namely:

  • Code of Civil Procedure, 1908
  • Punjab Courts Act, 1918
  • Indian Contract Act, 1872
  • Indian Sales of Goods Act,1930.
  • Indian Partnership Act, 1932
  • Specific Relief Act, 1963.
  • Transfer of Property Act 1882,
  • East Punjab Urban Rent Restriction Act 1949 and
  • Indian Evidence Act 1872.
  • Customary Law
  • Registration Act,1908.
  • Limitation Act,1963.
  • Indian Penal code,1860
  • Criminal Procedure Code , 1973
  • Hindu Law i.e. Hindu Marriage Act, 1955, Hindu Succession ACT,1956, Hindu Minority and Guardianship Act, 1956,and Hindu Adoptions and Maintenance Act, 1956
  • Mohammedan Law and Customary Law i.e.Muslim Women (Protection of Rights on Divorce) Act, 1986, Dissolution of Muslim Marriages Act, 1939, Muslim Personal Law (Shariat) Application Act, 1937 and Law of Inheritance.

Our academy serves 5 days in a week (on Saturday and Sunday it will be closed). It takes approximately 10-11 months to complete the whole syllabus for PCS (Judicial)/ HCS(Judicial) entrance exams. Interesting applicant who are willing to apply for the posts of PCS/HCS (Judicial) can join our academy and he/she will be taught all the PCS/HCS (Judicial) subjects within a specific period.

We expect that every student of our academy should remain punctual, disciplined, obedient and hardworking. They are also expected to go through and to follow the below noted principles of this academy

  • Please come for class at least 10 minutes earlier to the time fixed for the class.
  • Do not hesitate in asking your mentor, if any query arisen in your mind.
  • Listen to the lecture very attentively and with full concentration as we are going to make you capable for the post of a Judge.
  • Please switch off your cellphones before entering into the class till the class is over.
  • Please attend all the lectures as each and every lecture is interconnected and connected to all the topics in syllabus.
  • .You must have the book/Act/Code which is to be taught in the class.
  • Bring your notepad for noting down relevant material.
  • Feel that you are not now college student, rather you are going to become very responsible, wise, capable, efficient and effective candidate for competing for the post of a Judge.
  • Everyday, We will conduct a test based on lectures already delivered in the class.
  • We request all students not to deposit their fees unless they attend all lectures for first three consecutive working days in order to see whether this academy is having that calibre/quality/worth which they expect,because fee once paid will not be refunded.
